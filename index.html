About Us

The Mt Sinai Alumni Welfare Group is a community of former students who have come together to provide support during emergencies and times of loss. Our mission is to ensure that no member faces difficult times alone.

Established by alumni for alumni, we offer financial assistance, emotional support, and practical help when our members need it most. Through our collective efforts, we strengthen the bonds of our community and honor our shared experiences at Mt Sinai.

Membership is open to all Mt Sinai alumni who wish to contribute to this noble cause and be part of a supportive network that stands together through life's challenges.

How We Support

Emergency Assistance

Financial support during unexpected crises, medical emergencies, or natural disasters affecting our members and their immediate families.

Bereavement Support

Compassionate assistance and financial aid to members who have lost immediate family members, helping them through difficult times.

Community Events

Regular gatherings, reunions, and networking events to strengthen bonds and maintain connections within our alumni community.
